1. Introduction

Unity Elites ("we", "us", "our") operates this website and related subdomains. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have, in accordance with Google's Publisher Policies and applicable data protection laws.

2. Information We Collect

Depending on which page or subdomain you visit, we may collect:

  • Information you provide directly — such as your name, email address, or project details when you fill out a contact or client-intake form.
  • Usage data — such as pages visited, browser type, device type, and approximate location, collected automatically through standard web analytics on some of our subdomains.
  • Cookies and similar technologies — used by us and by third parties (including Google) as described below.

3. Google AdSense and Cookies

This site uses Google AdSense to display advertising. Google, as a third-party vendor, uses cookies (including the DoubleClick cookie) to serve ads based on your prior visits to this website or other websites on the Internet.

  • Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads to you based on your visit to this site and/or other sites on the Internet.
  • You may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings.
  • You can also opt out of some third-party vendor use of cookies for personalized advertising by visiting www.aboutads.info.
  • Third-party vendors, including Google, use cookies to serve ads based on a user's prior visits to our website or other websites.

We do not control the cookies placed by Google or other advertising partners and are not responsible for their individual privacy practices. We encourage you to review Google's own Advertising Privacy & Terms page for details.

4. Analytics

Some of our pages and subdomains may use standard web analytics tools (such as Google Analytics) to understand how visitors use our site. These tools may use cookies to collect anonymized or pseudonymized usage statistics. This data helps us improve site performance and content, and is not used to personally identify you unless you have separately provided identifying information through a form.

5. Forms and Client Data

If you submit information through a client intake form (for example, our project request form), we use that information solely to evaluate and respond to your request. We do not sell this information to third parties.

6. How We Use Information

  • To operate, maintain, and improve the website
  • To respond to inquiries and project requests

7. Your Choices

You can disable cookies through your browser settings at any time, though some site features may not function correctly as a result. You may also opt out of personalized advertising using the Google and industry-wide tools linked above.

8. Children's Privacy

This site is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.
