About Unity Elites

Who we are and what we build

Introduction to Unity Elites

Unity Elites is a small web development team focused on building responsive, professional websites and web tools — from front-end design through to hosting and deployment. We work across e-commerce builds, custom web applications, and supporting tools like our documentation hub and past-questions platform.

What We Do

What we do

We design and build responsive, interactive websites with a focus on clean UI/UX, solid performance, and good SEO fundamentals. Our stack spans Python, JavaScript, SQL, React, React Native, Django, and Laravel, and we handle front-end, back-end, cloud hosting, email routing, and DNS management for client projects.

Our Approach

  • Performance-first — we care about loading speed, efficient rendering, and clean error handling.
  • Security-conscious — SSL/TLS and secure defaults on everything we ship.
  • Practical tools — beyond client sites, we build and maintain our own tools (documentation, past-questions resources, and a learning bot) that we make freely available.

Why This Site Exists

This site is both our portfolio and our starting point for client projects. If you need a website built, our client intake form is the fastest way to reach us with your requirements.

Technologies We Use

  • Front-End: HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, React, React Native
  • Back-End: Python, Django, Laravel, PHP
  • Databases: SQL, MySQL, PostgreSQL
  • Hosting & Infrastructure: Cloud hosting, DNS management, SSL/TLS
  • Tools: Git, GitHub, VS Code, Figma
